Melanie Bancilhon

I am a second year CSE PhD student at Washington University in St. Louis working with Dr. Alvitta Ottley in the Visual Data Analytics Group (VDAG) at Washington University in St. Louis. I received my Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science and Architecture & Design at Smith College in Northampton, Massachusetts. My main research interests are at the intersection of visual analytics, human behavior and decision-making. My work focuses on how visualization can be used and adapted to encourage people to make better decisions.
